Difference between Padding, Border and Margin

Content

Content refers to the content of the box, where text and images appear. It is what we want to build padding, border or margin around.

Padding

Padding is in essence the space around our content and it is transparent. It also clears an area around the content

Border

Border is the actual border that goes around our content and our padding. It can have different colours and widths

Margin

Margin is outside the border and is also transparent. It provides space between our content and anything around it.
